# Artifact 1 Situation intent

## Situation

The Fellowship stands before the gates of Moria. They must decide whether to take the shorter but dangerous path through the mines or the longer, safer route around them. However, they lack reliable information about the current dangers inside Moria and along alternative paths.

Their food supplies are limited, and the journey ahead is long. They have already encountered enemies and are unsure how to avoid further threats. There is also a risk of getting lost, becoming separated, or being unable to communicate.

This situation is non-trivial because the Fellowship must make a decision under uncertainty, balancing risk, resource constraints, and incomplete information. The consequences of a wrong decision could be severe.

---

## Intent

The primary users are the members of the Fellowship (hobbits), who are cautious, inexperienced, and dependent on guidance.

The system is intended to support them in making informed decisions by providing relevant information about risks, routes, and resources, as well as maintaining communication if the group becomes separated.

The system does not make decisions for them. It provides guidance and recommendations, but the final decision always remains with the Fellowship.

---

## Value

The Fellowship gains increased safety and confidence in their journey by relying on structured information rather than intuition alone.

Without this system, decisions would be slower, less informed, and more prone to conflict. The risk of encountering enemies, running out of resources, or taking inefficient routes would significantly increase.

---

## System Capabilities

The system must enable the Fellowship to:

* understand their current situation, including location, risks, and available options
* evaluate alternative paths based on risk, distance, and resource consumption
* track and manage critical resources such as food and supplies
* maintain awareness of threats and potential dangers along the journey
* stay connected and share information, even if the group becomes separated

---

## Assumptions & Constraints

* Information about the environment is incomplete and may be outdated
* Updates about routes or dangers are not available in real time
* The Fellowship may be skeptical of the system and not fully trust its guidance
* Communication between members may be unreliable or temporarily unavailable
* The system operates under uncertain and changing conditions
