# Artifact 2 TFC: Decisions

## Selected System Capability

The selected capability is the Safe Route Decision System. It supports the Fellowship in choosing the safest and most efficient path towards their destination.

We chose this capability because decision-making under uncertainty is a critical challenge during the journey. The Fellowship must constantly balance speed, safety, and available resources.

At this stage of the journey, the risk of encountering enemies such as Nazgûl or orcs is high. Therefore, having structured support for route decisions is essential for survival and mission success.

## Design Rationale

### i. Support of Intent and Value

The design supports the Fellowship in making informed route decisions by presenting clear and structured information about available options.

It directly aligns with the system’s intent defined in Assignment 1: to assist the Fellowship in navigating uncertain and potentially dangerous environments. By highlighting key factors such as risk level and travel time, the system enables users to balance safety and efficiency according to their current situation.

This structured decision support reduces uncertainty, minimizes the likelihood of poor choices, and helps prevent conflict within the group by providing a transparent basis for decision-making.

### ii. Deliberate Omissions

We intentionally limited the scope of the design to a single core capability in order to maintain clarity and usability.

Additional features, such as advanced navigation elements (e.g. star-based guidance or magical connection systems), were deliberately excluded. Including such features would increase complexity and potentially distract from the primary interaction: selecting a safe and suitable route.

This decision follows the principle of prioritizing clarity over completeness.

### iii. Assumptions and Constraints

The design assumes that users may be under time pressure, operating in stressful or uncertain conditions, and may not have extensive experience with complex systems.

Therefore, we prioritized simplicity, clarity, and intuitive interaction. Only essential information (such as route options, risk levels, and travel time) is presented.

This ensures that the system remains usable and effective even in critical situations, allowing users to make quick and confident decisions.
