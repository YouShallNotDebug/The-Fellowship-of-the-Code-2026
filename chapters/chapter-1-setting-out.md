# The Red Book of Westmarch - Chapter I: Setting Out

## Summary

In this assignment, we defined the situation and intent of our system, the Fellowship Companion.
We described the challenges the Fellowship faces on their journey, especially the decision at Moria, and identified what kind of support the system should provide.

## Artifact

Link: [Artifact 1 - Situation intent](../artifacts/artifact-1/artifact-1-situation-intent.md)

In our artifact, we described:

- the situation (uncertainty, danger, limited resources)
- the intent of the system (supporting decision-making)
- the value of the system (more safety and better coordination)
- system capabilities and constraints

The goal was to create a clear conceptual foundation for the Fellowship Companion.

## AI Assistance

At first, we worked on the assignment without using AI.

Later, we used AI (ChatGPT) to:

- improve the clarity of our text
- simplify and structure our intent and capabilities
- better understand the difference between features and system capabilities

We noticed that:

- our initial version was too detailed and partly focused on features
- AI helped us make the descriptions more abstract and clearer

We reviewed and adjusted the AI output to match our original ideas.

## Lessons Learned

We learned that:

- defining the problem clearly is more important than jumping to solutions
- system capabilities should describe what the system enables, not specific features
- it is important to separate situation, intent, and value clearly

One challenge was not mixing up features (e.g., map, compass) with capabilities.

In the future, we would focus earlier on keeping our descriptions shorter and more abstract.
